Uttarakhand

Known as the "Land of the Gods", Uttarakhand is home to sacred temples, snow-capped peaks, and charming hill stations. It's a gateway to the Char Dham pilgrimage and a haven for trekkers and nature lovers.

Top Attractions:

Rishikesh, Nainital, Auli, Kedarnath, and Valley of Flowers.

Rishikesh

Nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas, Rishikesh is known as the Yoga Capital of the World. This spiritual town on the banks of the Ganges blends peaceful ashrams, sacred temples, and thrilling adventure sports like white-water rafting and bungee jumping—making it a haven for both soul seekers and adrenaline lovers

Auli

Auli is a pristine hill station in Uttarakhand famed for its snow-covered slopes and world-class skiing. Surrounded by oak forests and panoramic views of Nanda Devi and other Himalayan peaks, Auli is a winter wonderland that offers peace, beauty, and adventure all at once.
