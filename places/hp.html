Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh is a paradise for mountain lovers. Nestled in the Himalayas, it offers stunning landscapes, spiritual retreats, and adventurous activities like trekking and skiing.

Top Attractions:

Manali, Shimla, Spiti Valley, Dharamshala, and Kullu.

Manali

A charming resort town in Himachal Pradesh, Manali is surrounded by snow-capped mountains, lush valleys, and pine forests. It’s a favorite for backpackers, honeymooners, and adventure seekers, offering everything from river rafting to serene walks by the Beas River.

Shimla

Once the summer capital of British India, Shimla is known for its colonial architecture, scenic ridge, and toy train rides through the hills. With a blend of historic charm and mountain tranquility, it remains one of India’s most loved hill stations.
